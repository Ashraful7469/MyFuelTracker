⛽ MyFuelTracker
A modern, high-performance vehicle expense and fuel management app built with Jetpack Compose.

MyFuelTracker helps vehicle owners stay on top of their spending, fuel efficiency, and maintenance schedules through a beautiful, data-driven dashboard. No more spreadsheets—just clean, actionable insights.

✨ Key Features
Modern Gradient Dashboard: A "glassmorphism" inspired UI that provides a high-level overview of your vehicle's health and costs.
Fuel & Service Tracking: Log fuel fill-ups and maintenance tasks in seconds.
Automated Analytics: Instant calculation of cost-per-unit, lifetime spend, and fuel efficiency ($KM/L$ or $MPG$).
Multi-Vehicle Support: Seamlessly switch between motorcycles and cars with a custom vehicle manager.
Visual Data: High-fidelity pie charts to visualize your spending breakdown between fuel and service.
Material 3 & Jetpack Compose: Built with the latest Android standards for a smooth, native experience.
